More about O'Hare Airport

Need parking at the largest passenger airport in the state of Illinois? Well you’re in luck, ParkWhiz has got your back.

The third busiest airport in the world, Chicago O’Hare Airport (ORD) had more than 64 million passengers fly through in 2009. Over 2,000 flights per day going to and from more than 100 U.S. and international destinations also make O’Hare the world’s second busiest airport in terms of aircraft movement. Before 2007, it was voted “Best Airport in North America” for a decade by two top travel magazines.

Several O’Hare airport parking options are available to passengers via the City of Chicago Department of Aviation (CDA) which run the airport. A variety of parking including, hourly, daily, economy, and valet parking options are available. You can also park off-site at a private off-airport parking facility that’s only a short distance from the airport. In addition, many ORD airport hotels have their own parking or ‘park, sleep, and fly’ services available to ORD passengers.

With so many options, finding the perfect O’Hare parking option can be tough. Don’t fret! Here are a few tricks and tips to help you find the best option for you on this page:

Compare and check ORD parking rates. ParkWhiz makes this easy, just put in your arrival and departure dates and times into the timepicker at the top of the page. Once you’ve set your timeframe, the map will automatically show you all of the parking options available, with details and pictures to the left. Get the best available rate by comparing all locations at once.

Choose your ORD parking facility. With everything on one page, it’s easy to pick the best deal at the most convenient parking garage or lot. Click the location details to see the ins and outs of shuttle drop-off, pick up, and the facility’s amenities.

Reserve your parking space. Once you’ve made a choice, click the “book now” button to reserved your parking. Wasn’t that easy? You’re all set and ready to go with an ORD airport parking space.
